About the Workshop

Diffusion models have rapidly become the go‑to method for visual generation—yet they still face challenges such as slow sampling, limited user control, and difficulties handling long‑range temporal dependencies. This workshop brings together researchers pushing beyond diffusion to explore faster, more controllable, and more expressive generative paradigms for images, video, and 3‑D.

Join us for a full‑day program of keynote talks and discussions by leading academics and industry practitioners aimed at catalysing the next wave of breakthroughs in visual generative modeling.
